What car does Elijah Wood drive? Why is Elijah Wood Instagram private?

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Elijah Wood, a Lord of the Rings actor and acknowledged Anglophile, was 24 when he purchased his first Mini.

Even after eight years, the recollections are still vivid: He claims, “They’re like go-karts.”

They’re a lot of pleasure to drive because of how tightly and precisely they turn. You feel like you’re an extension of the automobile because of how rapidly they react to your moves and how little they are.

A 2004 road journey from Los Angeles to Rosarito, Mexico, for a Lord of the Rings reunion holiday with British co-stars Billy Boyd and Dominic Monaghan comes to mind without skipping a beat.

It was funny, he claims. “I traveled down with the enormous Samoan stuntman Sala Baker.”

What car does Elijah Wood drive?

He steers clear of showy vehicles and favors Audi and Mini Cooper vehicles.

Why is Elijah Wood Instagram private?

He hardly ever talks about his personal life and preserves his Instagram privacy settings.
