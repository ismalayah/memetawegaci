Publish date: 2024-06-17

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Artwork of Red wearing his sygna suit from the title screen

A sygna suit (Japanese: マジコス Serious Costume) is a special type of outfit appearing in Pokémon Masters EX that makes a Trainer match the appearance of their partner Pokémon. According to Brock, sygna suits are designed to give Trainers a stronger connection to their Pokémon. Wearing the outfit is said to make the Trainer feel as if they have become their Pokémon. Many sync pairs on Pasio involve a Trainer wearing a sygna suit to differentiate one sync partner Pokémon from their normal sync partner, and it is possible to scout for these Trainers to join the player's team.

In-universe, most sygna suits are designed by a famous fashion designer on Pasio, who makes the suits for free and often unprompted by the sync pair that inspired them. Some sygna suits, such as Lysandre's, are confirmed to be designed by someone else, but they are still designed with a specific sync pair in mind. Others, such as Acerola's and Lana's, were originally created for some other purpose, before the Trainer had paired up with the Pokémon who inspired the outfit's design. Olivia says these are "similar to sygna suits", not sygna suits themselves. However, these sync pairs are still treated the same as any other sygna suit sync pair.

The game credits list Emi Ando, Kyoko Abe, and Misaki Hashimoto of Game Freak as the designers of sygna suits.

Trivia

  • Red is the only Trainer whose sygna suit variant was introduced before their regular outfit.
  • Brock & Tyranitar are the only sygna suit sync pair with a base potential of 3★.
  • Red & Charizard were the first sygna suit sync pair capable of using Mega Evolution.
  • Leon & Eternatus were the first sygna suit sync pair with a Legendary Pokémon.
  • Leon & Eternatus were the first sygna suit sync pair capable of using Dynamax.
  • Lusamine & Necrozma were the first sygna suit sync pair capable of using a Z-Move.
  • Cyrus & Darkrai were the first sygna suit sync pair with a Mythical Pokémon.
  • Morty & Ho-Oh were the first sygna suit sync pair with a Shiny Pokémon.
    • They were also the first sygna suit sync pair with a Shiny Legendary Pokémon.
  • Morty & Ho-Oh were the first sygna suit sync pair with the Gym Leader theme skill, despite Morty not being the first Gym Leader who received a sygna suit.
  • Steven & Deoxys were the first sygna suit sync pair with the Champion theme skill, despite Steven not being the first Champion who received a sygna suit.
  • Cynthia & Lucario were the first sygna suit sync pair with a buddy move.
  • Diantha & Diancie were the first sygna suit sync pair with a Shiny Mythical Pokémon.
  • Red and Cynthia are the only Trainers with more than one sygna suit: Red has two, while Cynthia has three.
